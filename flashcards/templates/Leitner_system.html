Leitner system - why is it so effective?

The main principle of the Leitner method is to repeat the material often enough to remember it, and infrequently enough not to waste time on revising what has already been memorized. The necessary learning tools are flashcards and a box for flashcards with five compartments, which guarantees the optimal number of repetitions. (https://blog.fiszki.pl)
